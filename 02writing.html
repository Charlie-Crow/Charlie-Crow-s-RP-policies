GENRES

Fantasy. It’s the genre I’m the most familiar with because my very first stories and characters were in a Fantasy setting. Although it's not really the genre I'm craving for right now.

Supernatural, Urban Fantasy. Our world, our reality, but with supernatural turns and creatures, mystery and suspense. Vampires, werewolves, ghosts, and many other things we can talk about. It’s also a genre I’m very familiar with, I would be thrilled to RP that kind of stuff!

Sci-fi. I’m less familiar with that one, but man I enjoy RPing Sci-fi. I have several species I can include in a space opera RP, just ask!

Horror. I enjoy mystery, suspense, weird, monsters hiding in the dark, as well as the dark hiding in humans.

Modern, real life. I also enjoy a good slice-of-life RP, as long as romance or smut is not the absolute goal/central theme and if we come up with an interesting plot.


HEADWORLDS

Want to RP in one of my original settings? Let me tell you a few things about them.

Primordial Chaos.
My old-established, Fantasy story. It exists for at least 10 years.
Vast oceans. Endless hills and dunes. Deep mountains. Several nations, several powerful abilities that are deeply rooted in their nature. However, anyone using them is severely punished. In this world of opression and prejudice, ancient stories about the Primordial Five are whispered while the populations begin to face more and more mysteries; a giant roaming the coldest lands after a long, dreamless sleep, and a foreign traveler with very ominous markings staining her skin: Chaos' mark.

Code:MIRROR.
A Sci-fi, Cyberpunk setting. The story takes place on a futuristic Earth.
Technology is everywhere, in any aspect of people’s lives. It has never been more efficient and has never raised so many questions. However, it seems to be useless to understand or stop those strange, expanding blue zones appearing all around the world. This is foreign, this is Alien, this is destructive. And it is dangerous.

Cocoon.
Sci-fi, space opera.
Peyregora is a Terpsy mothership, a city-like vessel floating in outer space. A lot of explorers and scientists are on board, seeking answers and other secrets in the cosmos. But they’re not alone in those cold, infinite darkness; giant creatures, hostile planets, silent ships with crews of hunters… Everything one can imagine seems to exist at some point. You realize it when you come across your worst fear.
